# Spring Meadow Screensaver

A canvas-based spring meadow screensaver built on the mechanics from [Flying Air Fryers](https://github.com/h1ddenpr0cess20/flying-air-fryers). Reuses the bezier wing path, clipped feather/vein technique, and entity lifecycle patterns from the original.

## Features

- Rolling hills with trees rooted in the background landscape
- Dense wildflower meadow with 7 flower types (daisy, tulip, rose, sunflower, poppy, bellflower, wildflower) that bloom, age, and fade
- Natural tapered grass with wind sway
- Butterflies with separate forewing/hindwing anatomy and flower-resting behavior
- Birds (robin, bluebird, goldfinch, sparrow, cardinal) that fly, glide, and perch on tree branches
- Bees, ladybugs, and dragonflies that land on flowers
- Squirrels and rabbits on the ground
- Floating petals, dandelion seeds, and pollen particles
- Weather system cycling through clear, cloudy, rain, and thunderstorms with lightning
- Creatures flee during storms, stay during cloudy weather
- Sun, clouds, god-rays, and gentle sky color cycling
- Speed control slider

## Usage

Open `index.html` in a browser.
